Research

We are applied probabilists with interests in the modeling and analysis of stochastic systems, theoretical statistics, stochastic optimization and control. Current research interests include: 1. Applied Probability & Queueing Theory - Developed models for **non-stationary and transitory queues**, including diffusion approximations in oscillatory regimes. - Published work on **calibration of non-stationary queueing networks** and queue inference from indirect observations. ### 2. Stochastic Control & Reinforcement Learning - Developed foundations for **reinforcement learning in continuous-time systems**, including: - Impulse/discontinuous control - Optimal exploration policies - Gave invited talks (e.g., *Towards a Theory of Exploration in Continuous-Time RL*). ### 3. Controlled Markov Chain Estimation - Designed **adaptive, nonparametric minimax-optimal estimators** for transition densities in controlled Markov chains. - Derived **sample complexity bounds** and considered mixing properties and policy structures. ### 4. Optimization under Uncertainty & Bayesian Methods - Contributions to: - **Sample-average approximation** in stochastic optimization over function spaces - **Bayesian joint chance-constrained optimization** - **Distributed high-dimensional regression**
